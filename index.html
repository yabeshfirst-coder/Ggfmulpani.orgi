Who are we?
GGF Mulpani Church is a Bible-believing, non-denominational, and evangelical church. Also, this church believes in God the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit and constantly preaches Christ's grace and the completed work of Jesus Christ. We are committed with prayer to fulfill the great command of Christ and deliver the great mission transfer to all 77 districts of Nepal because this is a mission-minded church so that people who are lost without Christ can accept free salvation and be saved from the punishment of sin.
